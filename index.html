ABOUT

Hi there!
I am a fashion, food, and now a budding web developing enthusiast. I love doing creative things like painting, sewing and, picking roadside plants/flowers to create small displays. I have a background in content creation and used to film and photograph plant-based recipes for a blog. I also have a sales background and worked for a leading fabric wholesaler in NZ which sold beautiful, imported textiles from all over the world. Recently after a bit of a deep dive into web development and UX design I found I enjoyed the challenge in creating web experiences using code. I believe this would pair well with my background in design and help me create digital solutions that are unique and exciting.

WORK

Fashion Design
I have previously completed a Bachelor in Fashion Design (2018) at AUT. I continue to use the skill I mastered there to design a product that is fit for the brief. I have also received mentorship from Supriya Lele, a London based designer who further helped me develop my design ability.
